Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 18, 2025

At Tourista, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, app, and services.

1) Information We Collect
  • Personal details: Name, email, phone, billing address.
  • Payment details: Processed securely by trusted third-party providers (we never store card numbers).
  • Travel preferences: Destination choices, booking history.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, cookies for analytics.
2) How We Use Your Information
  • To process and confirm your bookings.
  • To provide customer support and travel updates.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To send promotional offers (only with your consent).
3) Sharing of Information
  • We may share data with tour operators, airlines, and hotels to fulfill your bookings.
  • We may share data with third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, analytics) under strict confidentiality.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to advertisers.
4) Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to personalize your experience, analyze site traffic, and measure marketing effectiveness. You may adjust cookie settings in your browser at any time, but some features may not function properly.

5) Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6) Your Rights
  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request a copy of your stored data.
7) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.

8) Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@tourista.example.
